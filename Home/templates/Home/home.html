Why smartbin

The Smart Recycling Bin aims to transform the current waste collection and management systems in Nairobi and grow to serve Sub Saharan Africa. The Smart recycling Bin is capable of identifying the type of waste being disposed of and rewards the user with points which they could later redeem for cash or discounts on products in participating stores or other commercial entities.
